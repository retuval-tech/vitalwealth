Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Welcome to VitalWealth ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and username
  • Authentication credentials (encrypted)
  • Device information and identifiers

Financial Data

  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses (public keys only)
  • Transaction history from connected wallets
  • Portfolio balances and asset allocations
  • DeFi lending and borrowing activity

Important: We never have access to your private keys, seed phrases, or the ability to move your funds. Your wallet connections are read-only.

Health Data

  • Heart rate, steps, and calories burned
  • Sleep duration and quality metrics
  • Blood oxygen (SpO2) levels
  • VO2 max and HRV measurements
  • Stress levels and recovery scores
  • Active minutes and workout data

Device Connections

  • Garmin Connect integration data
  • Apple HealthKit data (iOS)
  • Samsung Health data (Android)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate your Longevity Score based on health metrics
  • Determine insurance premium discounts based on health data
  • Generate your unified credit score combining DeFi history and health
  • Display portfolio analytics and transaction history
  • Provide personalized health insights and recommendations
  • Send notifications about loan repayments and policy updates

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive health and financial data
  • AES-256-GCM encryption at rest
  • Secure session management with Redis-backed persistence
  • WebAuthn/Passkey authentication support
  • Biometric authentication on supported devices
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

4. Blockchain Data

Your wallet addresses and on-chain transactions are processed through public blockchain networks. While blockchain data is inherently public, we implement privacy-preserving techniques to minimize exposure of your financial activities.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Insurance Partners: Anonymized health scores for premium calculations
  • Service Providers: Infrastructure and analytics partners under strict data protection agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Disconnect smartwatch integrations at any time
  • Remove wallet connections
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Health metrics are stored for up to 2 years to provide historical trends. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

VitalWealth is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of VitalWealth after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
