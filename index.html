Important: VitalWealth processes sensitive financial and health data. We employ bank-grade encryption and never sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and authentication credentials
  • Wallet Information: Public blockchain wallet addresses you choose to connect (we never access your private keys)
  • Health Data: Heart rate, sleep patterns, steps, VO2 max, HRV, stress levels, and other biometric data synced from connected smartwatches (Garmin, Apple Watch, Samsung Galaxy Watch)
  • Financial Data: Cryptocurrency holdings, transaction history, and DeFi positions across supported blockchain networks
  • Insurance Information: Policy details, claims history, and coverage preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Calculate your Longevity Score and biological age estimates
  • Determine insurance premium discounts based on health metrics
  • Generate unified credit scores combining DeFi history with health data
  • Process blockchain transactions and manage your portfolio
  • Send you notifications about your accounts, health insights, and loan repayments
  • Detect and prevent fraud, security incidents, and other harmful activities

4. Blockchain Data

Your wallet addresses and on-chain transactions are processed through public blockchain networks. While blockchain data is inherently public, we implement privacy-preserving techniques to minimize exposure of your financial activities. We never have access to your private keys or seed phrases.

5. Health Data Protection

Your health information receives the highest level of protection:

  • All health data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 encryption
  • Health metrics are processed locally on your device when possible
  • We comply with applicable health data regulations including HIPAA standards
  • You can delete your health data at any time through the app settings

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Insurance Partners: Anonymized health scores to calculate premium discounts (with your consent)
  • Blockchain Networks: Transaction data necessary to execute on-chain operations
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist in operating our services, bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

7. Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all sensitive data
  • Multi-signature wallet security for high-value transactions
  • Biometric authentication support
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Disconnect smartwatch integrations at any time
  • Export your data in a portable format

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, we may retain certain data for up to 90 days for backup purposes and as required by law.

10. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data transfers.
