Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to VitalWealth ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Important: VitalWealth processes sensitive financial and health data. We employ bank-grade encryption and never sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and authentication credentials
  • Wallet Information: Public blockchain wallet addresses (we never access your private keys)
  • Health Data: Heart rate, sleep patterns, steps, VO2 max, HRV, stress levels, and other biometric data from connected smartwatches
  • Financial Data: Cryptocurrency holdings, transaction history, and DeFi positions
  • Insurance Information: Policy details, claims history, and coverage preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Calculate your Longevity Score and biological age estimates
  • Determine insurance premium discounts based on health metrics
  • Process blockchain transactions and manage your portfolio
  • Send notifications about your accounts and health insights
  • Detect and prevent fraud and security incidents

4. Blockchain Data

Your wallet addresses and on-chain transactions are processed through public blockchain networks. We implement privacy-preserving techniques. We never have access to your private keys or seed phrases.

5. Health Data Protection

  • All health data encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256
  • Health metrics processed locally on your device when possible
  • We comply with applicable health data regulations including HIPAA
  • You can delete your health data at any time

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share only with:

  • Insurance Partners: Anonymized health scores (with consent)
  • Blockchain Networks: Transaction data for on-chain operations
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners bound by confidentiality
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

7. Data Security

We implement end-to-end encryption, multi-signature wallet security, biometric authentication, regular security audits, and SOC 2 Type II compliance.

8. Your Rights

You may access, correct, delete your data, opt out of marketing, disconnect integrations, and export your data at any time.

9. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active. After deletion, certain data may be kept up to 90 days for backups and legal requirements.
